European program with a specific action dedicated to promoting academic
mobility of students from more than 30 European countries
([KA103](http://www.upc.edu/sga/ca/Mobilitat/KA103)) and a specific
action dedicated to promotinig mobility of students between associated
countries not participating in the Erasmus+ program
([KA107](http://www.upc.edu/sga/ca/Mobilitat/KA107)).

**Conditions for the mobility of university students**

-   Students can make an Erasmus+ mobility per cycle of studies
    (Bachelor, Master, PhD).
-   The minimum length of stay is 3 months and the maximum 12 months.
    Therefore, you can make several trips in different academic years,
    provided they do not exceed 12 months in total.
-   You can make an academic mobility for studies (subjects and/or final
    thesis at a university) and/or an internship mobility (stay in a
    company).

Check how to [request a
place](/ca/estudis/secretaria/tramits/sollicitud-placa-de-mobilitat-erasmus-upc-europa-america-llatina-india-russia-taiwan-xina-i)
